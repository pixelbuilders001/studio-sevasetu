# OG Image Creation Instructions

## Required OG Image for Social Sharing

To enable proper preview images on WhatsApp, Facebook, Instagram, and Twitter, you need to create an OG (Open Graph) image.

### Specifications:
- **Size**: 1200 x 630 pixels (recommended ratio: 1.91:1)
- **Format**: PNG or JPG
- **File Name**: `og-image.png`
- **Location**: `/public/og-image.png`

### Design Guidelines:
1. **Background**: Use your brand color (#1e1b4b) or a gradient
2. **Logo**: Include your helloFixo logo prominently
3. **Text**: 
   - Main headline: "helloFixo"
   - Subheadline: "Your City's Most Trusted Doorstep Repair Service"
   - Tagline: "Premium Repairs at Your Doorstep"
4. **Visual Elements**: 
   - Include icons representing services (mobile, laptop, appliances)
   - Use professional, clean design
   - Ensure text is readable and high contrast

### Tools to Create:
- Canva (https://canva.com) - Search for "Facebook Post" template (1200x630)
- Figma
- Adobe Photoshop/Illustrator
- Online OG Image Generators

### Quick Steps:
1. Create a 1200x630px canvas
2. Add your logo and brand colors
3. Add compelling text about your service
4. Export as PNG
5. Save as `og-image.png` in the `/public` folder

### Alternative:
If you don't have an OG image yet, the system will fallback to `/logo-image.png`, but a custom OG image will look much better on social media!
